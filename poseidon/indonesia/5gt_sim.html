Simulations

In the past month I’ve been trying to collect as much economic data as possible about the boats. The idea is to coalesce all the various sources of data, use them for some basic simulation and make sure the numbers that come out are reasonable.

If they are reasonable, we just take them and plug them into POSEIDON. If they are not reasonable we go back and figure out which data source is wrong.

First we can take all boats \(\leq 6\text{ GT}\) and check on average how heavy their catches are.

It is clear that boats in the WPP 573 consistently land more than others.

If we take the expenditures per hour and trip length from that data-set (for small boats) we can generate random trip expenditures.
From CODRS we can take all the landings associated with small boats (excluding those from WPP 513 which is special), price them and use them to generate random landings.

Now we can generate random trip profits at will.

##     Min.  1st Qu.   Median     Mean  3rd Qu.     Max. 
## -7237927  -693516  1189344  1435100  3358274 11016052

Now imagine that each year people make between 6 to 9 trips a year and we get

##      Min.   1st Qu.    Median      Mean   3rd Qu.      Max. 
## -19362421   4920638  10460882  10742510  16314853  43277939

Then we have that on average a 5GT boat will make yearly profits of about 11M IDR. That’s quite low; if we assume that a 5GT boats costs 250M, its yearly upkeep will probably be about 50M.
