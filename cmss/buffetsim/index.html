Buffet Simulation, Slice 0

This page holds a simple agent-based model of buffet tables and food waste at a random restaurant.
It simulates 60 minutes at a very conceptual level, the agents act in purely robotic ways without any particular pretense of realism. However this model is here in order to test what kind of information and behaviour is needed to make this realistic. It is a demo.

Right now there are three parameters: how many tables are available in the restaurant, how many food stations (buffet tables with food) are available and after how many minutes a new group show up at reception waiting to be seated.
